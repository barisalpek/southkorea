Capital
The capital of South Korea is Seoul. Seoul is located in northwestern part of the country.
Population
The population of South Korea is 57.62 million (2018). 9 million of them live in Seoul
President
The president of South Korea is Moon Jae-In. He was elected in 2017 and the re-election is in 2022.
Language
In South Korea you obviously speak Korean as the main language. Koreans use the "Hangeul" alphabet system.
Nat. Flower
Mugunghwa (Rose of Sharon or Hibiscus syriacus).
Currency
1 South Korea Won equals 0.00082 USD.
Animal
National Animal of South Korea is the Siberian Tiger.
National Day
15 August is the National Holiday of South Korea.
